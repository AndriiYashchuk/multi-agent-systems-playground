# Does the USA invade Iran?

**Short answer:** **No confirmed U.S. ground invasion or occupation of Iran has been verified.**

At the same time, **yes — the U.S. is reported to be carrying out strikes and broader combat operations involving Iran.** So the most accurate answer is:

> **The U.S. is attacking/striking Iran, but there is no credible confirmation of a full ground invasion or occupation.**

## Definitions

- **Invasion / occupation:** U.S. ground forces enter Iran and **seize or hold territory**.
- **Strikes / military intervention:** air, missile, naval, cyber, or limited operations **without taking and occupying land**.

## What is confirmed

- **U.S. strikes on Iranian targets are confirmed.**
  - AP reported U.S. strikes on Iranian nuclear sites including **Fordo, Natanz, and Isfahan** in June 2025.
  - BBC also described those U.S. strikes and later 2026 **wide-ranging strikes on Iran**.
- **The conflict escalated further in 2026.**
  - Reporting from AP, Reuters, and BBC describes ongoing **U.S.-Israeli military operations against Iran** and Iranian retaliation.
- **No formal declaration of war is required for these strikes.**
  - BBC notes the president can order military action without a formal congressional declaration of war.

## What is not confirmed

I found **no credible AP, Reuters, or BBC confirmation** that the U.S. has:

- sent ground forces into Iran in a sustained invasion
- captured and held Iranian territory
- established an occupation inside Iran

So if you mean **“Did the USA invade Iran with troops and take land?”** the answer is **No, not based on currently verified reporting**.

## Latest developments relevant to the confusion

- Same-day and recent reporting indicates the U.S. is still involved in **active strikes and major combat operations**.
- Recent reports also discuss the **possibility of further escalation**, including statements about potential use of ground troops.
- But **discussion of possible troop deployment is not the same as a confirmed invasion**.

## Kharg Island

- Reporting supports **strikes on targets linked to Kharg Island**.
- I found **no verified evidence** from AP/Reuters/BBC that the U.S. **landed troops and seized Kharg Island**.
- So any claim that the U.S. has already **taken** Kharg Island should be treated as **unconfirmed**.

## Bottom line

**No — the U.S. has not been credibly confirmed to have invaded and occupied Iran.**

**Yes — the U.S. is reported to be striking Iran and participating in a wider conflict.**

That distinction is probably the key reason for confusion.

## Limits

This is a fast-moving situation. If new verified reporting confirms U.S. ground entry or territorial seizure, the answer could change.
